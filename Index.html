• Instructions and details (Read First)

    >> This page will help you to request Paramore in many radio stations at once. Just choose the country (and the group) and tweet! Check the radio stations database to see what group you should choose.


    >> Before anyone asks, you will be requesting "Ain't It Fun" everywhere.


    >> Each group contains 15 stations at max. If you want to tweet to more than one group of stations, please give a 15-minute break between the requests. Or else your account may get reported for spam on Twitter. Do NOT tweet to the same group more than once by day.


    >> For the record, we do not store any data about people who use this page. Also, let's make it clear: This page was not made to spam radio stations. We're just getting our tasks easier and faster to do! If something is not working well, please tweet me (@TheParamoreBand).
